Built A Multilingual Social Network That Drove Collaboration Among People From Different Geographies

Expertise: Web & Enterprise Portals Social Networking E-commerce Solutions

Driving collaboration among people belonging to different social group, the client manages a a robust multilingual online platform. This platform offers an extensive range of local services and social groups that help people collaborate better and for longer. This platform helps people connect on hobbies, food, health, demography, and foreign languages. Owning to its exceptional search capabilities, the platform helps users find, create, and share information according to their preferences faster and in an innovative format.
Business Needs
To accelerate its business growth and enable more people to connect with each other, the client wanted to design, develop, and maintain a multilingual social network online. The client had an idea of bringing this platform to the masses in a way that makes it easy to navigate and fast to embrace. The business needs of the engagement could be summarized in the following points.

  • Building an intuitive and feature-rich user interface for a social platform
  • Coming up with an end-to-end responsive web platform
  • Enabling the end-user to develop a service account
  • Empowering the end-user to add service-related details
  • Integrating a transparent and robust referral mechanism feature
Strategy & Solution
The client collaborated with Shaurya to realise its vision of a multilingual online platform. Shaurya and the client's business team collaborated on determining the core programming structure around which the platform would be developed. The development lifecycle began once the PHP and MySQL framework was completed. Using PHP web development and MySQL capabilities, the platform was designed so that users could easily and rapidly register an account, add services, and exchange essential information with no downtime.

Shaurya planned, produced, and deployed a variety of modules across the production and testing cycles to successfully roll out all platform features in phases. Shaurya used his UI and UX expertise, in addition to its programming staff, to create a lightning-fast and engaging multilingual platform.
