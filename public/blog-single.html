Developed An Intuitive Mobile App That Helped Create Memes For The Medical World

Expertise: Social Networking App
Verticals: Media & Entertainment
Technologies: Swift Android
The client manages an app that is designed to add humor to the medical world. The client teamed up with our experienced app developers to build an immersive and interactive app for creating memes, videos and stories while managing custom newsfeed.
Business Needs
The client wanted to bring humor to the dry medical world. Because of this goal, the client wanted an interactive app that streamlined the way medical-related memes, funny videos and stories are created by the users. To realize the goal, the client brought our team of hybrid app developers on board. The biggest challenge for our team was to build a one-of-its-kind app environment that made meme-creation for the medical field not only simple but also quick.
Strategy & Solution
  • We developed the app using best-in-class UX standards and development environments.
  • We created an extremely snappy app interface by keeping the touch latency to an all-time low.
  • We wrote PHP scripts for creating a watermark on every meme created.
  • The app was developed and delivered in React Native for both iOS and Android platforms.
Business Outcomes
  • We published an entertaining mobile app that refreshed the dry, serious world of medicine and healthcare through interestingly funny memes.
  • The interface of the app was interactive, fast and easy to use.
  • We built the app keeping in mind that load time is low.
  • Several optimizations to the app were done to make sure it remained visible on Google Play and App Store
