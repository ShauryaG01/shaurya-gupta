About Me

I enjoy collaborating with design teams to faithfully translate their concepts down to the last pixel. Every day, you'll find me utilising cutting-edge frontend technologies that bring the creative process to life just as designers intended.

Technical

Right now you can find me hacking away primarily with Flutter, Dart Nuxt.js, Gridsome, GraphQL and in general anything Android, iOS, JavaScript, HTML and CSS related. Also been known to dabble in React.js and Gatsby from time to time or wrestling with Webpack.

Personal

I grew up in Noida before relocating to Delhi a few years ago. When I have the time, I like to try my hand at boxing or go on solitary treks up in the mountains to meditate and reflect on how privileged I am to have this wonderful life. On most days, you can find me working in one of the neighbourhood coffee shops:)
