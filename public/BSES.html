Mobile App Modernization Enables A South Asian Energy Leader Boost Its App Adoption Rate By 60%

Expertise: Enterprise Application Business App
Verticals: Energy
Technologies: Java Xcode Android Studio Flutter IOS Dart
Since 2002, BSES has been a pioneer in implementing electricity distribution changes, establishing itself as a dependable utility provider committed to customer-friendly procedures. The client operates in one of South Asia's busiest metropolises, serving the energy consumption demands of an increasing number of inhabitants. Its broad activities have even resulted in the client's firm being listed on the Indian stock market. With a strong workforce of over 10,000 people, the client is constantly injecting automation and different digital products to enhance the way it provides energy solutions to customers. The client's operational revenue of more than USD $5 billion dollars has been generated via its continual focus to upgrading the experience of end-consumers.
Business Needs
The client had a business-level payment app for iOS and Android that lacked a human-centric appeal. To stay up with the digital era, the client sought to rebuild an existing mobile application that would take customer involvement to the next level. The customer had an idea to make the app more user-friendly by adding extra features.

  • Deploying a user-centered design experience
  • Transforming the bill-payment ecosystem
  • Delivering consumer-centric digital pay solutions
  • Integrating customer engagement initiatives
  • Simplifying user interaction with the app
Strategy & Solution
Shaurya worked with the client to upgrade their existing application. That is when the idea for the newer, more contemporary app was born. As a next-generation software, it comes with several capabilities for simplifying processes. The new BSES app was created with the consumer's every need in mind, allowing them to do anything from pay power bills to receive safety recommendations simply touching on their smartphone displays. Furthermore, Shaurya's capabilities reduced app load times and enabled users to manage their power accounts easily and swiftly through the app interface.

Front-End

Shaurya uses his app modernisation skills in iOS and Android to create an immersive UX that is also simple to use and intuitive. The client's team and Shaurya worked together to create the finest app design that pushed the edge in terms of increasing user engagement.

  • Incorporating an intuitive UI that is fast, responsive, and smooth
  • Bringing a host of functional add-ons for seamless navigation
  • Enhancing app usability by making the interface feature-rich
  • Adding multi-lingual support for improving UX and app adaptability


Backend

For making the app more streamlined, it had to completely sync with the backend operations. The backend development included long-term planning for improving app efficiency and minimizing the load-times. Here are the project highlights of the app's backend ecosystem.

  • Integrating new codes with legacy APIs
  • Implementing a payment gateway for secure transactions
  • Storing customer data on robust and well-protected databases
  • Accept and close support tickets, analytics and disputes resolution
  • Developing structured menus and dynamic screens


Development Process

Shaurya uses an agile app development methodology in which the app was gradually updated based on customer input and feedback. After each stage, the customer was provided an app build to the quality-control unit. Furthermore, the client was continually brought up to date on the most recent design and manufacturing advances. Client's team and Shaurya had a weekly sprint to discuss the previous week's milestones and to define development and modernization objectives for the next days. Overall, the development process was transparent, giving the customer comprehensive visibility throughout the app modernization cycle.

  • Gathering app requirements and brainstorming for new features
  • Setting development and production milestones periodically
  • Testing incremental app builds for upholding the highest quality standards
  • Extending post-delivery application support to bring the downtime
