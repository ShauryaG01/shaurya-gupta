Implemented A Cryptocurrency Exchange Using Alpaca For A Fintech Business

Expertise: Application Development
Verticals: Finance & Banking
Technologies: Angular JS Node.js Smart Contract Solidity Ethereum Hyperledger Web3JS Hyperledger Fabric MongoDB Express.JS
The client manages a fledgling cryptocurrency exchange, assisting investors in the purchase and sale of equities and crypto-assets. The exchange deals in a variety of crypto-assets, including Ethereum (ETH), Bitcoin (BTC), and Litecoin (LTC). The client is profiting on the disruption caused by blockchain, which will increase the growth potential for astute investors globally. The client's cryptocurrency exchange enables investors to obtain access to ground-breaking crypto goods and strategies. Even better, access is available for a fraction of the rate charged by industry leaders. The client has gone on a quest to assist individuals optimise their money with the opening of the exchange.
Business Needs
The client desired to create a complete cryptocurrency exchange and stock investment platform. This platform was designed to provide regular investors with access to top-tier crypto assets. Furthermore, the platform aided in the creation and management of a number of blockchain-based digital assets and stock investments. The website provides many investment options and includes a separate wallet that can be utilised for various humanitarian and social causes. The client also desired to create a platform with reduced transaction costs. Here are a few highlighted business requirements that the client had before the engagement began.

  • Designing and deploying an end-to-end crypto exchange and stock investment platform
  • Helping investors gain access to different crypto-based assets to maximize their wealth opportunities
  • Creating an ecosystem that would support frictionless transactions among different institutions
  • Minimizing the transaction costs when compared with what is charged by industry leaders
Strategy & Solution
The client sought to collaborate with a blockchain development partner to assist bring its vision of a full and robust crypto exchange from paper to reality. That's where we come in. Shaurya is a blockchain engineer with considerable expertise assisting businesses in the development of cryptocurrency exchanges from the bottom up. As a result of his experience, he became the go-to choice for assisting the client in developing a feature-rich crypto exchange. He collaborated with the client's business leaders and technical teams to identify the functionalities that exchange users will have. He decided, in collaboration with the client, to provide investors the option of purchasing and selling crypto-assets. In addition, Shaurya made it possible for customers to effortlessly transfer and withdraw their crypto-assets. Furthermore, he intended for the exchange to include an escrow payment wallet for BTC, ETH, and other top-performing assets. Similarly, he created an import crypto wallet to receive distinct crypto-assets from a separate wallet. Furthermore, the process of generating wallets for holding crypto-assets was intended to be simplified and accelerated. Shaurya had specialised user eKYC management tools in place to assist reduce the risk of fraud. Shaurya also used Kraken Exchange APIs to enable real-time trading. In addition, we leveraged Alpaca APIs to ensure real-time stock market investments occurred. Shaurya used AngularJS, Node.js, and MongoDB to construct the front-end, create the backend, and manage all database chores.

Front-End

Since the cryptocurrency exchange was supposed to attract hordes of investors, Shaurya makes sure the UI was interactive. That is why Shaurya used the latest components of HTML5/CSS and JavaScript. This way, our developers ensured that the UI had simplicity built right on the exchange's UI. Here are a few features that made the front-end of the project intuitive.

  • Prioritized usability of the exchange over everything else
  • Ensured the UI's learning curve was not steep
  • Built dedicated windows for helping investors trade
  • Streamlined the way sign-ups take place


Backend

Shaurya developers zeroed in on Node.js and Express.js to build a robust foundation for the application. He also used MongoDB as the database so that any transactional and data requests were handled optimally with zero downtime. Here are a couple of features for the application's backend.

  • Integrated Kraken Exchange APIs into the system for real-time crypto trading
  • Used Alpaca for simplifying how stock market investments take place
  • Leveraged Node.js and Express.js for developing APIs
  • Harnessed Accurascan for fast-tracking eKYC verification
  • Developed workflows to streamline and secure transactions
